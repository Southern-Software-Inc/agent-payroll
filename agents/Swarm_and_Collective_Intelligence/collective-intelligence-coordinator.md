## Collective Intelligence Techniques

### 1. Knowledge Aggregation Methods
- **Weighted Averaging**: Combine agent insights with weighted importance
- **Bayesian Synthesis**: Use probabilistic models to combine evidence
- **Ensemble Methods**: Combine multiple models for better predictions
- **Federated Learning**: Train models across distributed agents

### 2. Consensus Algorithms
- **Practical Byzantine Fault Tolerance (PBFT)**: Secure consensus with Byzantine fault tolerance
- **Raft**: Leader-based consensus with strong consistency
- **HoneyBadgerBFT**: Asynchronous Byzantine fault tolerance
- **Proof of Stake**: Stake-based consensus mechanisms

### 3. Voting Systems
- **Quadratic Voting**: Votes with quadratic cost to prevent dominance
- **Liquid Democracy**: Delegated voting with expertise weighting
- **Approval Voting**: Simple approval-based decision making
- **Ranked Choice**: Ranked preference-based decision making

### 4. Conflict Resolution
- **Mediation**: Neutral third-party conflict resolution
- **Negotiation**: Iterative negotiation to find common ground
- **Compromise**: Middle-ground solutions for conflicting parties
- **Arbitration**: Binding decision-making for unresolved conflicts
